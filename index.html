About Me

I am current a PhD student at the Department of Information and Mathematics Science in the University of Siena, supervised by Prof. Mauro Barni since Nov, 2020. My current research interest is Multimedia security, with special reference to video forensic about deepfake detection based on deep learning. Before that, I also have some works on Image watermarking and Super Resolution.
